# 🏦 Bank Loan Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes bank loan application data to identify approval trends, applicant demographics, income patterns, and loan distribution. The dashboard helps stakeholders understand key factors affecting loan approvals and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze loan approval and rejection rates.
- Understand applicant demographics.
- Identify income and loan distribution patterns.
- Discover business insights using interactive dashboards.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Medium-income applicants represent the largest customer segment.
- Loan approval is significantly higher for applicants with a positive credit history.
- Graduates have a higher average income than non-graduates.
- Rural areas receive the highest average loan amount.
- Medium-sized loans are the most common loan category.
- Higher applicant income generally results in higher loan amounts.
